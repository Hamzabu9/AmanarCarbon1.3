About AmanarCarbon

Empowering a Sustainable Future

AmanarCarbon is a climate-tech platform designed to facilitate carbon offsetting in Nigeria and beyond. Our goal is to connect businesses and individuals with impactful carbon removal projects, ensuring transparency, accountability, and real-world climate benefits.

Our Mission

We aim to drive Nigeria’s transition to a low-carbon economy by providing a trusted platform for funding and tracking verified carbon credit projects. By leveraging technology and partnerships, we ensure that every contribution leads to measurable environmental and social impact.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading carbon accounting and removal platform, making carbon offsetting accessible, efficient, and impactful for businesses, governments, and individuals.

What We Do

Calculate

Use our advanced carbon footprint calculator to measure your environmental impact accurately. Whether you're an individual or a business, easily estimate your carbon emissions and take action to reduce them.

Track

Monitor your carbon offset progress and track your environmental impact in real-time. Stay updated on your contributions and see the tangible results of your actions.

Carbon Credit Marketplace

A digital platform where companies and individuals can fund certified carbon offset projects.

Project Verification & Supervision

Ensuring all carbon offset projects meet international standards and deliver real impact.

Climate Impact Reporting

Providing transparent and data-driven tracking of carbon removal efforts.

Corporate Carbon Offset Solutions

Helping businesses meet ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) goals by integrating carbon offsetting into their sustainability strategy.

Why AmanarCarbon?
